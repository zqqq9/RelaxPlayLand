About This Game

Test your patience and precision by carving intricate patterns into honeycomb candy without breaking it. Inspired by the viral Squid Game challenge.

How to Play

Use your mouse or touch to carefully trace the pattern on the honeycomb candy. Move slowly and precisely to avoid breaking the candy. Complete the pattern within the time limit to win.

Features

Multiple difficulty levels, various patterns to choose from, time challenges, and realistic physics.
